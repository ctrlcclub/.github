# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Our pledge
We pledge to make participation in the community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Being respectful and kind in words and actions
- Giving and receiving feedback with empathy and clarity
- Crediting sources and respecting authorship
- Asking for consent before sharing someone else's work or private messages

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language
- Sharing private information without permission
- Plagiarism or claiming others' work as your own
- Trolling, baiting, or deliberate disruption

## Enforcement
If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the maintainers by opening a private report using the contact information in the repository or by emailing the maintainers. We will review every report and take appropriate action.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces and all contributions in this repository.
