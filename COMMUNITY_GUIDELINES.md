# Community Guidelines

ctrlcclub is a community for builders who learn by copying, improving, and shipping. These guidelines explain how we collaborate and keep the space healthy.

## Principles
- Learn in public and share what you are building
- Credit sources, inspirations, and collaborators
- Improve what you copy, and share what you change
- Help others by giving clear, actionable feedback
- Keep discussions constructive and respectful

## Sharing work
- When you share a project, include links to sources or inspirations when applicable
- If you adapt someone else's work, explain what you changed and why
- Ask before reposting someone else's work outside the community

## Feedback etiquette
- Be specific and focus on the work, not the person
- Offer suggestions and examples when possible
- Accept feedback with curiosity and gratitude

## Moderation
Maintainers may remove content or members who violate the Code of Conduct or these guidelines. Repeated violations can lead to temporary or permanent removal.

## Contact
If you need help or want to report an issue, contact the maintainers via the repository contact details.
